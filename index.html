Save Our Spiders
A lot of people are afraid of spiders because they may be poisonous. But there are over 45,000 species of spiders, and only about 25 are harmful. Out of those 25 species of spiders, most live in South American and Australia.
So some of the people who are scared or frightened may kill the spiders even if it is the typical house spider in the U.S.
My message to all people is to SAVE OUR SPIDERS!.
